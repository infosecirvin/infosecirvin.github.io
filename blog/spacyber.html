Are you interested in taking cybersecurity classes in Spanish? If so, we need your input!

First Class: Security+

This course introduces fundamental principles and topics of Information Technology Security and Risk Management at the organizational level. Addresses hardware, software, processes, communications, applications, and policies and procedures.

Class Structure

The class is broken down into 16 weeks, aligning with out typical college semester. It contains video lectures, hands-on labs on various platforms such as Google Cloud, National Cyber League, Netlab, TryHackMe and collaboration via Discord.

Students would participate in NCL ($35) and TryHackMe (free; but there is a $10/month upgrade available)

Price: $150

This price is just about the same as our Community College courses, without the extra fees usually found when taking a college course.
