Security+

Esta clase introduce los temas fundamentales de seguridad informatica y manego de riesgo al nivel de la organizacion. Cubre equipo fisico, digital, procesos, comunicacion, programas, policias y procedimientos. Esta clase prepara estudiantes para tomar el CompTIA Security+ examen. Are you interested in taking cybersecurity classes in Spanish? If so, we need your input! We are looking for enough interest to get this off the ground.

Security+

This course introduces fundamental principles and topics of Information Technology Security and Risk Management at the organizational level. Addresses hardware, software, processes, communications, applications, and policies and procedures. This class prepares students for the CompTIA Security+ Exam. Students can receive college credit later when they sign up at Cabrillo College.

Class Structure

The class is broken down into 16 weeks, aligning with our typical college semester. It contains video lectures, hands-on labs on various platforms such as Google Cloud, National Cyber League, Netlab, TryHackMe and collaboration via Discord.

Students would participate in NCL ($35) and TryHackMe (free; but there is a $10/month upgrade available)

Price: $150

This price is just about the same as our Community College courses, without the extra fees usually found when taking a college course.
